# PitchPerfect 2026: Tactical Roadmap & Development Plan

This document outlines the strategic evolution of the PitchPerfect platform from a simple tracker to an AI-powered tactical agent.

The MVP is designed for the modern athlete who wants to log data immediately after a match with zero friction.

### Core MVP Features:
- **Neural Capture Engine**: Use Gemini 3 Flash to process natural language text or voice recordings into structured match data.
- **Hybrid Data Entry**: A dual-mode system offering a "Magic" AI portal and a "Manual" precision form.
- **Local-First Performance Vault**: 100% private data persistence using browser storage.
- **Link-up Intelligence**: Tracking goals, assists, teammate names, and "assisted-by" credits.
- **Performance Analytics**: Instant calculation of total goals, assists, and average neural rating.
- **Export capability**: Raw CSV export for external spreadsheet analysis.

## ðŸ—º Strategic Roadmap

### Phase 1: Foundation & Frictionless Entry (Current)
*Focus: Data Integrity and Voice/Text AI Processing.*
- [x] Neural text extraction from plain English logs.
- [x] High-fidelity voice recording and processing.
- [x] Teammate and Link-up play tracking.
- [x] Local-first storage and basic performance stats.

### Phase 2: Tactical Awareness (Q3 2025)
*Focus: Environmental Grounding and Visual Insights.*
- [ ] **Climate Grounding**: Use Gemini Search grounding to automatically fetch weather data for match dates/locations to see how weather affects performance.
- [ ] **Visual Pitch Mapping**: Implementation of a "Goal Map" where users tap on a pitch graphic to mark where actions happened.
- [ ] **Trend Analysis**: 10-match rolling average charts for ratings and goal contributions.

### Phase 3: Team Synergy (Q4 2025)
*Focus: Relational Intelligence.*
- [ ] **Chemistry Analytics**: AI analysis to identify your "Best Partner" (e.g., "You score 3x more often when playing with Sarah").
- [ ] **Squad Profiles**: Automatic aggregation of stats for teammates you frequently play with.
- [ ] **Opponent Scouting**: Historic data lookup for teams you've played before to suggest tactics.

### Phase 4: The AI Tactical Coach (2026)
*Focus: Proactive Coaching & Career Growth.*
- [ ] **Tactical Debriefs**: Post-match AI feedback suggesting improvements based on match comments.
- [ ] **Scouting Resume**: Generate a professional PDF "Performance CV" summarizing career stats for scouts or coaches.
- [ ] **Advanced Forecasting**: Predictive ratings based on training logs and rest patterns.
